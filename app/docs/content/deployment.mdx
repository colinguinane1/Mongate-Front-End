# Front End Deployment

There are many ways to deploy your front-end application. Here are a few options:

## Vercel - (Reccomended)

Vercel is a great option for deploying your front end. It is free and easy to use. You can deploy your front end with a few clicks.

[Vercel](https://vercel.com/)

## Netlify

Netlify is another great option for deploying your front end. It is also free and easy to use. You can deploy your front end with a few clicks.

[Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/)

## AWS

AWS is a more advanced option for deploying your front end. It is not free, but it is very powerful. You can deploy your front end with AWS Amplify or S3.

[AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/)

## Heroku

Heroku is another option for deploying your front end. It is free and easy to use. You can deploy your front end with a few clicks.

[Heroku](https://www.heroku.com/)

[Railway](https://railway.app/)

## Heroku

Heroku works for front-end and back-end

[Heroku](https://www.heroku.com/)

## AWS

You can deploy your back end with AWS Amplify or EC2.

[AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/)
